# AI Writing Detection Checklist

Work through these categories in order. Use exact tags shown.

---

## Content Tells

**[INFLATED]**
"stands as," "serves as," "is a testament to," "plays a vital/crucial/pivotal role," "underscores the importance," "highlights the significance," "indelible mark," "deeply rooted," "profound heritage"

**[SYMBOLISM]**
"symbolizes," "reflects broader," "embodies the spirit of," "represents a turning point," "enduring legacy"

**[NOTABILITY]**
Listing media outlets as proof of importance; "featured in," "cited by," "independent coverage," "active social media presence"; sections cataloging where subject was mentioned

**[SUPERFICIAL-ING]**
Present participle phrase tacked onto fact: "...ensuring," "...reflecting its importance," "...highlighting," "...underscoring," "...contributing to broader"

**[PROMO]**
"continues to captivate," "groundbreaking," "stunning," "nestled in the heart of," "boasts a," "world-class," tourism/commercial tone

**[CHALLENGES-FUTURE]**
"Despite its [positive words], [subject] faces challenges..."; "Future Outlook" sections; vague optimism after listing problems

**[ECO-FILLER]**
Generic ecosystem role statements; conservation padding when status unknown; unsubstantiated preservation claims

---

## Language Tells

**[AI-LEX]**
additionally (sentence-initial), align with, crucial, delve, emphasizing, enduring, enhance, foster, garner, highlight (verb), interplay, intricate, key (adj), landscape (abstract), multifaceted, pivotal, realm, showcase, spearhead, tapestry (abstract), testament, transformative, underscore (verb), utilize, valuable, vibrant

**[NOT-ONLY-BUT]**
"Not only X, but Y"; "It's not just about X, it's about Y"; "However" pivots creating artificial balance

**[RULE-OF-3]**
"Adjective, adjective, and adjective"; tripled phrases for false comprehensiveness

**[VAGUE-ATTR]**
"Experts argue," "Observers note," "Industry reports suggest," "Scholarship describes"—without named source

**[WEASEL]**
"some," "many," "various," "several" without quantifier or citation when specificity expected

**[ELEGANT-VAR]**
Same entity called by 3+ synonyms in close proximity to avoid repetition (e.g., "the company," "the firm," "the organization," "the enterprise")

**[FALSE-RANGE]**
"From X to Y" where X and Y aren't on coherent scale; meaningless figurative ranges

---

## Structural Tells

**[TITLE-CASE]**
Section Headings With Every Main Word Capitalized

**[INLINE-BOLD]**
Boldface inside sentences for emphasis; "key takeaways" style bolding

**[INLINE-LIST]**
Vertical list items formatted as: **Bold header:** followed by description

**[EM-DASH]**
Em dash where comma, colon, or parentheses expected; multiple em-dash pairs per paragraph; em dashes for "punchy" parallelism

**[CURLY-QUOTES]**
"Curly" quotation marks or apostrophes ' instead of "straight" ones '

**[MARKDOWN]**
## headings, **bold**, *italic*, [link](url), bullet characters (•, -, –) instead of proper markup

**[EMOJI]**
Any emoji in running text or headings

---

## Communication Artifacts

**[DIRECT-ADDRESS]**
"In this article, we will explore"; "It's important to note"; "worth remembering"; didactic asides

**[LETTER-FORMAT]**
"Dear Wikipedia Editors," "I hope this message finds you well," "Subject:" lines

**[PLACEHOLDER]**
2025-xx-xx, [Enter Name], [Insert Date], bracketed instructions

**[KNOWLEDGE-CUTOFF]**
"as of my last update," "specific details are limited," "based on available information," "not widely documented"

**[COLLAB]**
"Would you like me to...," "Here is a...," "I hope this helps," "Let me know if..."

**[REFUSAL]**
"As an AI language model," apologies for limitations

---

## Citation Artifacts

**[SOURCE-EMPHASIS]**
Painstaking attribution of trivial facts; "According to [source]" for uncontroversial information

**[OAICITE]**
:contentReference, oaicite, turn0search, utm_source=openai, utm_source=chatgpt.com

**[REF-BUG]**
Reference declared but never called; malformed citation syntax

**[LINK-ROT]**
External URL likely broken (404); unarchived dead link

**[ISBN-DOI-FAIL]**
Invalid ISBN checksum; DOI resolves to unrelated paper
